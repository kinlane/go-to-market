KubeCon is the flagship conference of the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF), held annually (and regionally) to bring together developers, platform engineers, DevOps professionals, and ecosystem partners who build and operate Kubernetes and related cloud-native technologies.

The event serves as a global gathering point for the open-source cloud-native community, offering keynotes, technical sessions, tutorials, and panels on topics such as Kubernetes, containers, microservices, observability, networking, and security. It is often co-hosted with CloudNativeCon, reflecting the broader CNCF ecosystem beyond just Kubernetes.

## Topics
- Kubernetes Core and Operations
- Platform Engineering & Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs)
- AI, ML, and Data Workloads
- Security, Compliance, and Policy
- Cloud-Native Application Development
- Networking and Service Connectivity
- Observability, Telemetry, and Operations
- Multi-Cloud, Edge, and Hybrid Deployments
- Open Source Projects and Ecosystem Innovation
- Culture, Community, and Sustainability

Apidays Paris is a technology conference focused on APIs, digital platforms, and the "API economy". It brings together developers, architects, and business leaders to discuss how APIs drive innovation and cover topics like AI-ready APIs, data security, digital sovereignty, and the intersection of APIs with cloud-native technologies and sustainability.  

- Focus: The conference centers on the strategic and technical aspects of APIs, exploring how they are transforming industries and enabling new business models. 
- Topics: Key areas of discussion include developing and managing APIs for AI applications, ensuring data security and governance, and navigating issues of digital sovereignty. Recent events have also focused on cloud-native architectures, event-driven architectures, and the role of APIs in green technology. 
- Audience: It is designed for a wide range of professionals, including developers, architects, tech decision-makers, and business leaders who work with APIs. 
- Format: The event includes keynotes, panel discussions, and networking sessions. It is part of a global series of apidays conferences held in major tech hubs.
